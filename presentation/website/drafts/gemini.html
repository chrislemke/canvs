Product Vision

Spatial Primitives

We are not building a map app. We are creating a Spatial Browser consisting of new digital-physical artifacts.

Bubbles

Shared containers anchored to a place. "We used to meet here every Friday." Group memories that live on location.

Time Capsules

Rich artifacts hidden until a specific date or until you return. A letter to your future self, locked to a coordinate.

Trails

Narrative routes woven through the city. "My break-up walk," or "Architecture of 1920." Curated paths.

Deep Tech Foundation

Anchor Runtime V1
Multi-tier Anchoring GPS drift breaks immersion. We utilize Visual Positioning Systems (VPS) and local SLAM to lock memories to physical geometry.
