Logo Concept: "The Prism Pin"

  • 1
    The Form: A modified map pin. Standard pins point at a location. The CANVS pin is an aperture.
  • 2
    The Window: The center is not empty; it contains the gradient of the "Aether" (Indigo/Orange). It symbolizes looking through the glass to see the hidden layer.
  • 3
    The Softness: No sharp angles. The shape is organic, like a polished stone or a drop of water, fitting the "Earth" theme.
MVP v1 Definition

Proving the "Place Pull"

The MVP is not about AR glasses (yet). It is about intent and movement. The core loop requires users to physically travel to a location to "unlock" content.

GPS-Only (v1)

We use browser Geolocation API. Accuracy is ~10-20m. We visualize this uncertainty with "Accuracy Circles" rather than precise pins.

The Unlock Mechanic

Content is "Blurred" until the user enters the Geofence (e.g., 50m radius). This gamifies the journey.
